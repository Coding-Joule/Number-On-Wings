The Story So Far

Germany fought bravely for 120 minutes. After falling behind in the first half, they equalized and even thought they had found the winning goal. Unfortunately, VAR ruled it out because of a foul earlier in the play.

The match went to extra time, where both teams created chances but neither could find the decisive goal. Finally, the game was settled by a penalty shootout.

Germany missed two penalties, while Paraguay held their nerve and won 4–3. Although Manuel Neuer made an incredible save, it was not enough to keep Germany in the tournament.

A New Mission

Now all Germany fans have a new favorite team: France. The next challenge is clear:

🇫🇷 France, Help Germany Get Revenge! 🇩🇪

France will face Sweden in the Round of 32. If France win, they will meet Paraguay in the Round of 16.

We hope France can continue their winning form and defeat Paraguay. Not because Paraguay played badly—they earned their victory—but because football fans always dream of seeing the team that knocked out their favorite get knocked out next!
